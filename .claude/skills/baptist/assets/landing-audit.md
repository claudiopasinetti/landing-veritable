## 7-Dimension Audit

### 1. Clarity (Value Proposition)

**Question**: Can a visitor understand what this is and why they should care within 8 seconds?

- [ ] Value proposition is visible above the fold
- [ ] Headline communicates core benefit (not feature)
- [ ] Written in customer language (not company jargon)
- [ ] Specific and differentiated (not "best solution for your needs")
- [ ] Subheadline reinforces or extends the headline
